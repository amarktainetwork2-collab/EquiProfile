# EquiProfile — Features Guide

## Dashboard

- Overview of all your horses, upcoming tasks, recent activity
- Weather widget showing riding suitability for your location
- Quick links to add health records, training sessions, and documents
- Standard Dashboard for individual owners; Stable Dashboard for professional yards

## Horse Profiles

- Create a profile for each horse: name, breed, age, colour, microchip number
- Upload a profile photo
- Link health records, training sessions, and documents directly to the horse
- View full history at a glance

## Health Records

- Vaccinations — track due dates, get email reminders before they expire
- Dental care — log dental appointments and next-due dates
- Hoof care — record farrier visits
- Treatments — medications, vet visits, injuries
- X-rays — upload and store X-ray images securely
- Deworming — log treatments and schedules

## Training & Competition

- Log training sessions with date, duration, discipline, notes, and performance score
- Track competition entries and results
- Create structured training programme templates
- Lesson scheduling for trainers/instructors

## Calendar

- Monthly grid view and agenda/list view
- Create, edit, and delete calendar events
- Events include: training, competition, veterinary, farrier, lesson, meeting, and other
- Tasks with due dates and appointments also appear as read-only overlays
- Colour-coded by type for quick scanning

## Tasks

- Create to-do items with title, description, type, priority, and due date
- Assign tasks and set status (pending, in progress, completed, cancelled)
- Tasks with due dates appear on the Calendar automatically

## Appointments

- Schedule vet, farrier, dentist, physio, trainer, or other appointments
- Track provider details, location, cost, and status
- Appointments appear on the Calendar automatically

## GPS Ride Tracking

- Start a live GPS ride to record your route on a real-time map
- Track distance, duration, speed, and max speed
- Save completed rides with notes and link to a horse
- View ride history and replay routes on a map
- Ride data is auto-saved to your device during tracking so it is not lost if you navigate away

## Contacts

- Store details for vets, farriers, trainers, suppliers, and other contacts
- Quick access to phone numbers and email addresses

## Feeding & Nutrition

- Log daily feed costs and quantities
- Create nutrition plans per horse
- Track feed cost trends over time
- Nutrition logs for daily intake records

## Documents

- Upload any document (PDF, image, Word) up to the plan quota
- Secure storage — only you and your team can access files
- Download at any time
- Supported: PDFs, images (JPEG, PNG, WEBP), Word docs

## AI Weather Analysis

- Powered by Open-Meteo (no API key required for basic weather)
- Enter your stable location to get current weather and a 7-day forecast
- AI analyses conditions and gives a riding suitability score: excellent / good / fair / poor / unsafe
- Warnings for extreme heat, cold, wind, heavy rain
- AI analysis requires an API key to be configured by the admin (optional enhancement)

## AI Chat Assistant

- In-app AI assistant for horse care, training, and management questions
- Knows the EquiProfile platform and can guide you to the right section
- Quick-action buttons to create tasks or calendar events directly from the chat

## Breeding Management

- Record breeding dates, stallion details, gestation tracking
- Log foal information and pedigree

## Pedigree

- Visual pedigree tree for each horse
- Import/export pedigree data

## Equine Passport

- Digital equine passport record per horse
- Store passport number, FEI ID, UELN, and issuing authority

## Tags

- Create colour-coded tags to organise and label horses, records, and items

## Stable & Team Management (Stable plan)

- Invite team members by email
- Assign roles: Owner, Manager, Staff, Viewer
- Role-based access control — staff see only what they need
- Shared horse records across the whole team
- Stable setup and staff management tools

## Client Portal

- Share a read-only view of a horse's records with owners or clients
- No EquiProfile account required for portal viewers

## Analytics & Reports

- Activity charts: training frequency, health events, feed costs over time
- Generate and download PDF/CSV reports
- Schedule automated reports by email
- Training, health, and cost analysis dashboards

## Messages

- In-app messaging between team members

## Settings

- Profile settings (name, email, password change)
- Notification preferences
- Language (multiple languages supported)
- Theme (light / dark)
- Billing management — upgrade, downgrade, cancel, view invoices

## Documents Troubleshooting

- **Upload fails** — check file size (plan quota applies) and file type (PDF, images, Word only)
- **Download fails** — ensure you are logged in; session may have expired
- **File not found** — file may have been deleted; contact support
